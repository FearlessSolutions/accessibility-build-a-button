Exercise 1 - If it looks like a button...

...and acts like a button, it's probably a button.

The first step is to take a component and make it look like a button. You'll be using the same code for your button throughout this workshop, so get creative! You can find a skeleton for your button under src/custom-button.js. The stylesheet for your component can be found at src/custom-button.scss.
